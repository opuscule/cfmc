Welcome to The Carrboro Family Medicine Center.

Carrboro Family Medicine Center is a well-established, independent practice with a lab, and other diagnostic amenities. We are committed to serving our patients and being a positive change in healthcare by delivering timely, high quality, cost-effective medical services while also providing a great experience for our patients.

Our History

Carrboro Family Medicine Center, P.A. was founded in 1983 by Doctor James Manor. The practice has been caring for all family members from newborn infants to elderly grandparents ever since we opened our doors.

Our Mission

Our Mission at Carrboro Family Medicine Center is to provide patient-centered healthcare for individuals and families. To achieve complete satisfaction for each patient is our goal.

Our Vision

The direction of Carrboro Family Medicine Center is toward a more efficient, effective delivery of comprehensive healthcare based on traditional medical values assisted by 21st century technology.

Our Providers

Carrboro Family Medicine is an established, independent practice committed to providing excellence in Family Medicine to our patients. We are growing to provide greater access to care and welcome new patients to our practice. We look forward to serving you and your family for all your healthcare needs. Carrboro Family Medicine puts patients first.
